About TopoScout

This tool helps you analyze terrain to find highest points, calculate maximum ascent, visualize slope steepness, and overlay GPX routes on the map. The app is designed to work on mobile devices as well.

GitHub Project / droidgren.github.io

Version: 2.13.1 (Build 2977)

Changelog
  • v2.13.1: Fixed an ordinary refresh sometimes loading an older build than the latest — in any browser, not just the installed app. The service worker was ignoring the version stamp on the app's scripts and styles and serving a cached copy; a normal refresh now reliably loads the newest build, with no hard reload needed.
  • v2.13.0: The footer now shows the current map Scale (e.g. 1:50 000) and, while GPS is on, the Center to GPS distance — the straight-line distance from your GPS position to the map crosshair — both stacked under the Zoom value. Each readout can be shown or hidden individually under Advanced settings.
  • v2.12.0: You can now install TopoScout to your Home Screen on iPhone & iPad — the "Install as App" button now appears in Safari and shows simple step-by-step "Add to Home Screen" instructions.
  • v2.11.0: The app now updates itself automatically — no more tapping "Update". When a new version is ready it refreshes in the background and shows a brief "Updated" note, so the home-screen app (especially on iPhone) is always on the latest build.
  • v2.10.0: Added an optional Contour lines overlay. Turn on "Enable contour line layer" under Advanced settings to draw topographic contour lines over the terrain, with elevation labels along the major contours — toggle those separately with "Enable contour labels". The contour interval and labels follow your Metric / Imperial choice.

  • Tap "Add" — TopoScout appears on your Home Screen.
